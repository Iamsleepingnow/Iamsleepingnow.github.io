  • 当今元宇宙发展可能出现了悖论。拿“去中心化”举例，他们应当去考虑一下“阻力”问题。月球上的羽毛和铁块几乎是同时落地的，但是在地球上不行。当空气密度或乌托邦系统足够大时，阻力就会来撂倒这个系统。这样的系统对于工业生产十分有利，但是对高度社会化、政治化的社交系统而言，是不适配的。

    这种理想化的作法如同一场仪式。在仪式过程的阈限状态中，所有人都知晓接下来将会发生的事，并且能够合力完成。“去中心化”的人们对于仪式目标如同机器般绝对信任，绝无出现任何偶然的差错。

  • There is a possible paradox in the development of today's meta-verse. Take "decentralization" as an example, they should consider the "resistance" issue. Feather and iron on the moon fall to the ground almost simultaneously, but not on Earth. When the air density or utopian system is large enough, the resistance comes to subvert the system. Such a system is great benefit to industrial production, but it is not suitable for social systems that are highly social and political.

    This idealized practice is like a ritual. In the liminality state of the ritual process, all people know what will happen next and can work together to accomplish it. The "decentralized" people have absolute trust in the goal of the ritual as if it were a machine, without any chance of error.
