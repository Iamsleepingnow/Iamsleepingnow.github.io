彭艺伟

Peng-Yiwei

尝试使用视觉交互、影像、声音等多媒体媒介手段来探讨事物的人类学价值。作品围绕现实与幻想、现在与非现在、消费与生产、数据与社会分配、诞生与归宿进行凝练；分析与提倡道德与价值观，但不局限于软性的去当下话题。

Try to use visual interaction, video, audio and other multimedia methods to explore the anthropology value of things. Creations of Yiwei Peng were revolve around and condense in reality and fantasy, present and non-present, consumption and production, data and social distribution, birth and destination. Advocating the construction of morality and values, but not limited to the soft non-current topic.
